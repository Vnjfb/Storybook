Types of Distribution Systems

Distribution systems can be classified into radial, ring, and interconnected systems. Each system has its own advantages and disadvantages.

  • Radial System: Simplest design, used for low-cost rural areas.
  • Ring System: Offers redundancy, used in urban areas.
  • Interconnected System: Highly reliable, suitable for high-load demands.
