Load Flow Analysis

Load flow analysis is crucial for understanding the power flow through a system. It helps in planning and optimizing power systems.

Methods:

  • Gauss-Seidel Method
  • Newton-Raphson Method
  • Fast Decoupled Load Flow

Fault Analysis

Fault analysis is performed to determine the impact of faults (short circuits, open circuits) on the power system. It ensures system protection and reliability.

Types of Faults:

  • Symmetrical Fault
  • Unsymmetrical Fault
  • Single Line-to-Ground Fault
  • Line-to-Line Fault

Applications of Power System

Power systems are critical in the functioning of:

  • Electrical grids for power generation, transmission, and distribution.
  • Smart cities and renewable energy integration.
  • Industrial automation and energy-efficient systems.
