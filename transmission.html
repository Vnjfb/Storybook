Types of Transmission Lines

Transmission lines are classified into short, medium, and long lines based on their length and operating voltage.

  • Short Transmission Line: Up to 50 km, low voltage.
  • Medium Transmission Line: 50-150 km, medium voltage.
  • Long Transmission Line: Above 150 km, high voltage.
