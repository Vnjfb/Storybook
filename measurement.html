Error Analysis

In measurements, errors are inevitable. They can be classified into:

  • Systematic Errors: Caused by faulty instruments or methods.
  • Random Errors: Occur due to uncontrollable factors.
  • Gross Errors: Caused by human mistakes.
Understanding and minimizing these errors are crucial for accurate measurements.

Electrical Measuring Instruments

Electrical measuring instruments include:

  • Voltmeter: Measures the voltage between two points.
  • Ammeter: Measures the current flowing through a circuit.
  • Multimeter: A versatile tool that can measure voltage, current, and resistance.
